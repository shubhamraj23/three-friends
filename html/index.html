The Three Friends

A walk down the memory lane

Change is the only permanent thing in the world. As time flies by, people change; their behaviour changes. The Three Friends is one such story of three childhood friends, who went through such changes with time. Will their friendship be able to surpass these changes? Or will time break the strong bond and emerge victorious?
